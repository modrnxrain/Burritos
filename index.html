Plan your next Vermont Vacation in Vermont's Northeast Kingdom! Nestled between the Green Mountains and the Connecticut River in northern Vermont, Vermont's Northeast Kingdom offers breath-taking scenery, outstanding lodging and dining, ecotourism and agritourism, Vermont made products, a rich diversity of art, and recreational opportunities which have gained the Northeast Kingdom national and international recognition.


   

Vermont’s Northeast Kingdom is revered by residents and visitors alike for its lovely countryside, abundant natural resources and the preservation of traditional landscapes and lifestyles that have made the Vermont experience one to be cherished.

"..this could very well be the most beautiful place in America"

Patricia Schultz, Author of 1,000 Places to See Before You Die


Start planning your vacation here! Our Kingdom Spotlight highlights businesses, events and attractions that visitors will not want to miss when visiting the Northeast Kingdom.


Northeast Kingdom Byway

Travel Ideas

The 51-mile Northeast Kingdom Byway offers recreation, notable dining and lodging, quintessential Vermont villages, enduring farmland, and a rich diversity of art, shops, entertainment, and events all year long.

Spice up your journey to Vermont's Northeast Kingdom with cultural events, stunning landscapes, recreation, or a look back in time. The Northeast Kingdom offers something special for everybody.


Farm-to-Table Dining

Local Agriculture

Vermont’s Northeast Kingdom is a place where “farm to table” was never a trend—it’s a lifestyle. Nearby farms supply restaurants with local beef, pork, and chicken for a true 'farm-to-table' dining experience.

Explore Incredible Local Foods

Local food production has always been a part of the Kingdom’s culture, but now its popularity brings new visitors from all over the world to find culinary inspiration. We’ve preserved our agricultural traditions while also finding the means to earn a living in our remote and beautiful corner. Learn more about Northeast Kingdom local foods.
